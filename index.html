Praatalign: an interactive Praat plug-in for performing phonetic forced alignment

Praatalign is a plug-in for Praat that can be used to do forced phonetic alignment on speech signals and in particular free speech. Praatalign combines the powerful HTK toolkit with the interactivity and modularity of Praat to create an interactive, intuitive and extendable application.
